Overview

COR-HRM, developed by Symplore, is an advanced, AI-driven Human Resource Management (HRM) solution designed to streamline and enhance HR processes for businesses of all sizes. With modules that support every stage of the employee lifecycle—from recruitment and onboarding to performance, payroll, and exit management—COR-HRM empowers HR professionals to manage their workforce efficiently and strategically.

COR-HRM
Features & Benefits

Recruitment & Onboarding

COR-HRM simplifies the hiring process with automated resume screening, candidate tracking, and seamless communication tools. The onboarding feature ensures a smooth start for new employees, complete with digital document management, task assignment, and progress tracking.

Learn More

Employee Database

Maintain a centralized, secure employee database that houses comprehensive employee profiles, job history, personal information, and more. This feature enables quick access to employee records and supports compliance and reporting needs.

Learn More

Leave & Attendance Management

COR-HRM’s leave management system offers flexible, customizable leave policies, while the attendance module allows for real-time tracking of working hours and shift schedules, supporting remote and hybrid work environments.

Learn More

Performance Management

Enhance employee development with COR-HRM’s performance management tools, featuring goal setting, feedback loops, 360-degree reviews, and automated performance analytics to identify top talent and support career growth.

Learn More

Payroll & Compliance

The payroll module handles end-to-end payroll processes, including salary calculation, tax compliance, and statutory deductions. COR-HRM is compliant with local regulations and easily integrates with other accounting systems.

Learn More

Employee Self-Service

Empower employees with self-service tools for tasks like requesting time off, updating personal details, and accessing pays Slips, promoting a self-sufficient, engaged workforce.
